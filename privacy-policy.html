Privacy Policy

Learn how we protect your privacy and handle your data

Last updated: November 2023

1. Introduction

PicsPanda is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we handle your data when you use our services.

2. Information We Don't Collect

Our services process all files directly in your browser. We do not:

  • Upload your files to any server
  • Store your files
  • Track personal information
  • Use cookies for tracking

3. Browser Processing

All file processing happens locally in your web browser. Your files never leave your device.

4. Analytics

We use basic anonymous analytics to understand tool usage patterns and improve our services.

5. Contact

For privacy questions, contact us at: privacy@picspanda.com
