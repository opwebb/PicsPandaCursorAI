About PDF Rotate

Our PDF Rotate tool allows you to rotate pages in your PDF documents to the correct orientation. You can rotate all pages, specific pages, or odd/even pages by 90, 180, or 270 degrees. This is useful for fixing scanned documents, preparing PDFs for printing, or improving readability. All processing happens directly in your browser - your files never leave your computer, ensuring maximum privacy and security.

How to Rotate PDF Pages

  1. Upload your PDF file by dragging and dropping it or clicking the "Choose File" button.
  2. Select which pages you want to rotate (all pages, selected pages, page range, or odd/even pages).
  3. Choose the rotation angle (90° clockwise, 180°, or 90° counter-clockwise).
  4. Customize the output filename if needed.
  5. Click the "Rotate PDF" button to process your file.
  6. Download your rotated PDF document.

Features

  • Rotate all pages or specific pages in a PDF
  • Multiple rotation angles (90°, 180°, 270°)
  • Visual page selection with thumbnails
  • Options for rotating odd or even pages only
  • Preview of the document before and after rotation
  • 100% free with no watermarks
  • Private and secure - no server uploads
