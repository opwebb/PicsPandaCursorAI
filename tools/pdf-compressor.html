About PDF Compressor

Our PDF Compressor tool helps you reduce the file size of your PDF documents while preserving quality. This is perfect for sharing via email, uploading to websites, or saving storage space. All processing happens directly in your browser - your files never leave your computer, ensuring maximum privacy and security.

How to Compress PDF Files

  1. Upload your PDF files by dragging and dropping them or clicking the "Choose Files" button.
  2. Select the compression level based on your needs.
  3. Adjust the image quality slider to balance size and quality.
  4. Choose whether to remove metadata for additional size reduction.
  5. Click the "Compress PDF" button to process your files.
  6. Download your compressed PDF documents.

Features

  • Multiple compression levels to suit your needs
  • Adjustable image quality settings
  • Option to remove metadata for additional size reduction
  • Batch processing for multiple PDF files
  • 100% free with no watermarks
  • Private and secure - no server uploads
