About Image Compressor

Our Image Compressor tool helps you reduce the file size of your images while maintaining visual quality. This is perfect for websites, email attachments, or saving storage space. All processing happens directly in your browser - your files never leave your computer, ensuring maximum privacy and security.

How to Compress Images

  1. Upload your image files by dragging and dropping them or clicking the "Choose Files" button.
  2. Choose your compression mode:
    • Compress by Quality: Adjust the compression level slider - higher values mean smaller file size but potentially lower quality.
    • Compress to Target Size: Enter a specific target file size in KB that you want your images to be compressed to.
  3. Choose whether to maintain the original format or convert to a more efficient format.
  4. Click the "Compress Images" button to process your files.
  5. Download your compressed images individually or as a ZIP archive.

Features

  • Reduce file size by up to 90% while maintaining good quality
  • Two compression modes: by quality level or to a specific target size
  • Adjustable compression levels to balance size and quality
  • Option to maintain original format or convert to more efficient formats
  • Batch processing for multiple files
  • 100% free with no watermarks
  • Private and secure - no server uploads
