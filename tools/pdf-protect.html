About PDF Protect

Our PDF Protect tool allows you to add password protection to your PDF documents. You can set a user password that's required to open the document, an owner password that restricts permissions, or both. This is useful for securing sensitive documents, protecting intellectual property, or controlling who can edit or print your PDFs. All processing happens directly in your browser - your files never leave your computer, ensuring maximum privacy and security.

How to Protect PDF Files

  1. Upload your PDF files by dragging and dropping them or clicking the "Choose Files" button.
  2. Select the protection type (user password, owner password, or both).
  3. Enter the password(s) you want to use.
  4. For owner password protection, select which permissions to allow.
  5. Customize the output filename if needed.
  6. Click the "Protect PDF" button to process your files.
  7. Download your password-protected PDF documents.

Features

  • Add user password to restrict who can open the document
  • Add owner password to control permissions
  • Restrict printing, copying, editing, and annotations
  • Batch processing for multiple files
  • Strong encryption for maximum security
  • 100% free with no watermarks
  • Private and secure - no server uploads

Security Note

All password protection is performed directly in your browser. Your passwords and documents are never sent to our servers, ensuring maximum privacy and security. However, please note that PDF password protection has limitations and can be broken by specialized software. For highly sensitive documents, consider using additional security measures.
