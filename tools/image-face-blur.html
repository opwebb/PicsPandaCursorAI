About Face Blur

Our Face Blur tool automatically detects and blurs faces in images to protect the privacy of individuals. This is useful for sharing photos on social media, creating documentation, or complying with privacy regulations. All processing happens directly in your browser - your files never leave your computer, ensuring maximum privacy and security.

How to Blur Faces in Images

  1. Upload your image files by dragging and dropping them or clicking the "Choose Files" button.
  2. Select the blur method (Gaussian blur, pixelation, or black box).
  3. Adjust the blur intensity and face detection sensitivity.
  4. Choose the detection mode (faces only, faces and eyes, or custom areas).
  5. Click the "Blur Faces" button to process your files.
  6. If needed, verify detected faces or manually select areas in the editor.
  7. Download your processed images individually or as a ZIP archive.

Features

  • Automatic face detection using advanced algorithms
  • Multiple blur methods (Gaussian blur, pixelation, black box)
  • Adjustable blur intensity and detection sensitivity
  • Manual selection option for custom areas
  • Interactive editor for verification and manual selection
  • Batch processing for multiple files
  • 100% free with no watermarks
  • Private and secure - all processing happens locally
