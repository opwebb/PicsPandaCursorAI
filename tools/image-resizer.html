About Image Resizer

Our Image Resizer tool allows you to resize images to specific dimensions or by percentage. This is useful for preparing images for websites, social media, or printing. All processing happens directly in your browser - your files never leave your computer, ensuring maximum privacy and security.

How to Resize Images

  1. Upload your image files by dragging and dropping them or clicking the "Choose Files" button.
  2. Choose the resize method: dimensions (pixels) or percentage.
  3. Enter the desired width and height, or scale percentage.
  4. Choose whether to maintain the aspect ratio of the original image.
  5. Click the "Resize Images" button to process your files.
  6. Download your resized images individually or as a ZIP archive.

Features

  • Resize by exact dimensions or percentage
  • Maintain aspect ratio option to prevent distortion
  • Support for multiple image formats
  • Batch processing for multiple files
  • 100% free with no watermarks
  • Private and secure - no server uploads
