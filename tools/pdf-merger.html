About PDF Merger

Our PDF Merger tool allows you to combine multiple PDF files into a single document. This is useful for creating comprehensive reports, combining related documents, or organizing your files. All processing happens directly in your browser - your files never leave your computer, ensuring maximum privacy and security.

How to Merge PDF Files

  1. Upload your PDF files by dragging and dropping them or clicking the "Choose Files" button.
  2. Rearrange the files by dragging them in the desired order.
  3. Select the merge method (append or interleave).
  4. Enter a name for the output file.
  5. Click the "Merge PDFs" button to process your files.
  6. Download your merged PDF document.

Features

  • Combine multiple PDF files into a single document
  • Rearrange files in any order before merging
  • Choose between append and interleave merge methods
  • Customize the output filename
  • 100% free with no watermarks
  • Private and secure - no server uploads
