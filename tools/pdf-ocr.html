About PDF OCR

Our PDF OCR (Optical Character Recognition) tool converts scanned PDF documents into searchable, selectable, and editable text. This is perfect for digitizing paper documents, making scanned books searchable, or extracting text from image-based PDFs. All processing happens directly in your browser - your files never leave your computer, ensuring maximum privacy and security.

How to Make PDFs Searchable

  1. Upload your PDF files by dragging and dropping them or clicking the "Choose Files" button.
  2. Select the language that matches the text in your PDFs.
  3. Choose the OCR mode based on your accuracy needs.
  4. Select your preferred output type.
  5. Enable auto-rotate, deskew, and image enhancement options for better results.
  6. Specify a page range if you only want to process certain pages.
  7. Click the "Process PDF" button to start the OCR.
  8. Download your searchable PDF documents.
