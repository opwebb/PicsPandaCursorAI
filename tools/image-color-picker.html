About Color Picker

Our Color Picker tool allows you to extract colors from images and get their color codes in various formats. You can pick individual colors by clicking on the image or extract a color palette from the entire image. This is useful for web design, graphic design, and other creative projects. All processing happens directly in your browser - your files never leave your computer, ensuring maximum privacy and security.

How to Use the Color Picker

  1. Upload your image file by dragging and dropping it or clicking the "Choose File" button.
  2. Select your preferred color format (HEX, RGB, or HSL).
  3. Choose the number of colors to extract for the palette.
  4. Click the "Extract Colors" button to process your image.
  5. Click anywhere on the image to pick a specific color.
  6. View the color codes and copy them to your clipboard.
  7. Browse the extracted color palette below the image.

Features

  • Pick colors from any point in the image
  • Extract dominant colors to create a palette
  • Get color codes in HEX, RGB, and HSL formats
  • Copy color codes to clipboard with one click
  • Support for multiple image formats
  • 100% free with no limitations
  • Private and secure - no server uploads
