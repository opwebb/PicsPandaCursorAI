About Text Recognition (OCR)

Our Text Recognition tool uses advanced AI-powered Optical Character Recognition (OCR) to extract text from images. This is perfect for digitizing printed documents, extracting text from screenshots, or making scanned documents searchable. All processing happens directly in your browser - your files never leave your computer, ensuring maximum privacy and security.

How to Extract Text from Images

  1. Upload your image files by dragging and dropping them or clicking the "Choose Files" button.
  2. Select the recognition language that matches the text in your images.
  3. Choose the recognition mode that best suits your content type.
  4. Select your preferred output format.
  5. Enable auto-rotate and image enhancement options for better results.
  6. Click the "Extract Text" button to process your files.
  7. Download the extracted text in your chosen format.
