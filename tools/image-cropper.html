About Image Cropper

Our Image Cropper tool allows you to remove unwanted parts of your images and focus on the important elements. You can crop using predefined aspect ratios or create custom crops. All processing happens directly in your browser - your files never leave your computer, ensuring maximum privacy and security.

How to Crop Images

  1. Upload your image files by dragging and dropping them or clicking the "Choose Files" button.
  2. Select a crop method: Custom, Square, 16:9, 4:3, or 1:1.
  3. Click the "Crop Images" button to process your files.
  4. For custom cropping, you'll be able to select the area after clicking the button.
  5. Download your cropped images individually or as a ZIP archive.
