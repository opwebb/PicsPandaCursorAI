About Image Watermark

Our Image Watermark tool allows you to add text or image watermarks to your photos to protect your copyright and prevent unauthorized use. You can customize the watermark text, font, color, position, and opacity. All processing happens directly in your browser - your files never leave your computer, ensuring maximum privacy and security.

How to Add Watermarks to Images

  1. Upload your image files by dragging and dropping them or clicking the "Choose Files" button.
  2. Select the watermark type: text or image.
  3. For text watermarks, enter your text and customize font size and color.
  4. For image watermarks, upload a watermark image (preferably with transparency).
  5. Choose the watermark position and adjust opacity.
  6. Click the "Add Watermark" button to process your files.
  7. Download your watermarked images individually or as a ZIP archive.

Features

  • Choose from multiple watermark positions
