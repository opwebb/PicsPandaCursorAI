About Background Remover

Our Background Remover tool uses advanced AI algorithms to automatically detect and remove backgrounds from your images. This is perfect for creating product photos, portraits, or any image where you need to isolate the subject. All processing happens directly in your browser - your files never leave your computer, ensuring maximum privacy and security.

How to Remove Backgrounds

  1. Upload your image files by dragging and dropping them or clicking the "Choose Files" button.
  2. Select the output format (PNG and WEBP preserve transparency).
  3. Choose the detection mode that best matches your image content.
  4. Adjust the edge refinement slider for smoother or more precise edges.
  5. Click the "Remove Background" button to process your files.
  6. Download your images with transparent backgrounds.
