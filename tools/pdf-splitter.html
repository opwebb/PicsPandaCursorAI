About PDF Splitter

Our PDF Splitter tool allows you to split large PDF files into smaller documents. You can split by page ranges, extract each page as a separate PDF, split into even and odd pages, or split every N pages. This is useful for sharing specific sections of a document, extracting pages for printing, or breaking down large files. All processing happens directly in your browser - your files never leave your computer, ensuring maximum privacy and security.

How to Split PDF Files

  1. Upload your PDF file by dragging and dropping it or clicking the "Choose File" button.
  2. Select the split method that suits your needs.
  3. For page range splitting, enter the ranges you want to extract.
  4. For N-page splitting, specify how many pages per output file.
  5. Customize the output filename pattern if needed.
  6. Click the "Split PDF" button to process your file.
  7. Download your split PDF files individually or as a ZIP archive.
