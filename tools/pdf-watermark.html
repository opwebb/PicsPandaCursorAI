About PDF Watermark

Our PDF Watermark tool allows you to add text or image watermarks to your PDF documents. This is useful for protecting your intellectual property, marking documents as drafts or confidential, or adding branding to your PDFs. All processing happens directly in your browser - your files never leave your computer, ensuring maximum privacy and security.

How to Add Watermarks to PDF

  1. Upload your PDF files by dragging and dropping them or clicking the "Choose Files" button.
  2. Select the watermark type: text or image.
  3. For text watermarks, enter your text and customize the font, color, and size.
  4. For image watermarks, upload a transparent PNG image (recommended).
  5. Adjust the opacity, position, size, and rotation of the watermark.
  6. Choose which pages to apply the watermark to.
  7. Click the "Add Watermark" button to process your files.
  8. Download your watermarked PDF documents.

Features

  • Add text or image watermarks to PDF documents
  • Customize watermark appearance (opacity, size, position, rotation)
  • Choose from multiple font options for text watermarks
  • Apply watermarks to specific pages or the entire document
  • Preview watermarks before applying
  • Batch processing for multiple PDF files
  • 100% free with no restrictions
  • Private and secure - all processing happens locally
